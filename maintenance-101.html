Maintenance 101

What is Maintenance 101? Great question! A major motivation for me working on this site is to assist others in learning the trade of mechanical maintenance in an industrial environment. I need to be clear and up front about this. I am just a person trying to help other people. I do have experience training people but this isn’t a college course, more like a good beginners guide, a How to get started, a what to expect at the start.

The sections I include here are intended to show you what you can expect when you first apply for a maintenance position somewhere. Maybe you’re getting hired on as a new hire or maybe you’ve been a production type employee looking to move up to a higher paying maintenance job. Either way this area of the site is specifically tailored to you. The rest of the site will also prove useful, but expect it to be more in depth there. As time goes on I will include various maintenance tests I find from real plants as well as tests I’ll make up myself which are representative of what you’ll encounter.
